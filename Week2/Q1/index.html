About Us

Incepted in the year 2007, “Sukhmani Enterprises” is one of the leading Manufacturer firms of Electric Air Cooler and Desert Air Cooler. We have recruited a staff of qualified, experienced, and knowledgeable professionals, who are well-trained to understand and fulfill the diversified demands of patrons, within the promised time frame. These products are manufactured using the best quality raw material and the latest technology at our well-established infrastructure unit. The offered range is well known for its unique features such as durability, optimum performance, low maintenance, maximum cooling capacity, and long service life.
